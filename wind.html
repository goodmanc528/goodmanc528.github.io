Latitude vs. Wind Speed

When comparing latitude to wind speed we did not see any trends that jumped out. We were able to make some observations, however. Most cities in the data, at any laititude, did not observe wind speeds above 15 mph. There were two noticable outliers in the dataset with both of those cities reporting wind speed near or above 30 mph. Another five cities were near the 25mph mark. It may be worth the time and effort to see if these cities are connected in some manner: global positioning, location repective to current jetstream patterns, etc.
