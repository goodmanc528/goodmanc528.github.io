Latitude vs. MaxTemp

No real surprises were found when looking at the maximum temperature for cities. Those cities closer to the equator had higher maximum temperatures which is what was expected before the analysis began.

An observation of note is the cities highest maximum temperature do note reside exactly on the equator. Those cities are found between -20 and 0 degrees latitude and 20 to 40 degrees latitude. This is most likely attributed to the Earth's axial tilt - the angle between the Earth's rotational axis and its orbital axis.
