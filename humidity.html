Latitude vs. Humidity

In this analysis it was seen that cities closer to the equator, while smaller in number, generally do not have a humidity level below 40%. Other cities in the dataset, some a great distances from equator, had a high humidity level. This tells us that proximity to the equator is not a good indicator of the humidity level you would expect to find in a given city.
